What We Do

Our Programs

Six interconnected pillars of support designed to remove every barrier between a student and their potential.

Comprehensive Support

School-Based Services That Work

Our partnership model reinforces instructional quality, supports teachers, and improves student performance through coordinated academic, material, and wellness supports.

📖

Academic Support & Tutoring

Our academic support program reinforces instructional quality through direct tutoring, project-based learning materials, and enrichment programming. We work alongside teachers to ensure students receive the individualized attention they need to succeed.

One-on-one and small group tutoring
Project-based learning materials
After-school enrichment programming
Homework help and study support
Teacher resource support
💻

Technology Access

In a digital world, lack of technology is a critical barrier. We supply computers and tablets to partner school classrooms, and work to expand internet access for students who lack reliable connectivity at home — ensuring no child is left behind by the digital divide.

Classroom computers and tablets
Home internet access support
Digital literacy instruction
STEM-based learning activities
🧠

Counseling & Wellness

Our certified counselors and partner clinicians offer individual and group counseling, trauma-responsive services, and family support to meet the social, emotional, and mental health needs of students. A child's emotional wellbeing is inseparable from their academic success.

Individual counseling sessions
Group counseling and peer support
Trauma-responsive care
Family support services
Crisis intervention resources
🍎

Nutritional Support

Food insecurity is one of the most significant barriers to learning. We collaborate with schools to provide meals and nutritional support during and beyond the school day — because a child cannot concentrate, learn, or grow on an empty stomach.

Meal programs in partner schools
Weekend and holiday food support
Community food partnerships
Nutrition education programming
👗

Essential Supplies & Basic Needs

Students experiencing poverty often face daily challenges — lacking proper clothing, hygiene essentials, or emergency supplies — that make it impossible to focus on school. We ensure every student can arrive ready to learn.

Clothing and uniforms
Hygiene and personal care supplies
School supplies and backpacks
Emergency essentials for families
🚀

Workforce Readiness & Mentorship

By integrating workforce readiness, STEM-based learning, and mentorship opportunities into partner schools, we expand students' access to viable career and post-secondary pathways — ensuring today's at-risk youth become tomorrow's leaders.

Career exploration and job shadowing
Mentorship matching
STEM workshops and activities
Post-secondary pathway planning
Teen parent resources
