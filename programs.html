STEM Workshops

Our STEM workshops bring hands-on learning directly into partner schools, helping students build confidence through technology, collaboration, and real-world problem solving. These sessions are designed to be active, engaging, and memorable.

Each workshop creates a dynamic learning experience that introduces students to problem-solving, digital tools, and collaborative project work in a way that feels practical, exciting, and accessible.

School Nutrition Support

Our Student Nutrition Program provides supplemental meals to students through donated food from local grocery stores, restaurants, and community partners. View our "Thank You" page to see some of our donors, and a special thank you to them!

Meals are provided directly on campus and offered at no cost to students. Our program enhances the school day with additional nutritious options and occasional special meals, fostering a sense of celebration, community, and student well-being.

Additional Services

📖

Academic Support & Tutoring

Direct tutoring, project-based learning materials, and enrichment that reinforce classroom instruction and help students succeed.

💻

Technology Access

Computers, tablets, and connectivity support that expand digital access for partner schools and families.

🧠

Counseling & Wellness

Counseling, trauma-responsive support, and family services that strengthen student wellbeing and school engagement.

🍎

Nutritional Support

Meal support and food partnerships that reduce hunger and help students stay focused throughout the school day.

👗

Essential Supplies & Basic Needs

Clothing, hygiene essentials, backpacks, and emergency supplies so students can show up ready to learn.

🚀

Workforce Readiness & Mentorship

Career exposure, mentoring, and pathway planning that open doors to post-secondary success and long-term opportunity.
