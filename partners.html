Special Recognition

Special Thanks to Our Recent Partners

A special thank you to Jacqueline Johnson from Starbucks and Tamiyl Bradford from ShopRite for their generous donations of coffee and breakfast trays during our workforce development visit at OIC CADI. Their support helped create a welcoming space for students as representatives from Fairmount Water Works shared valuable insights, career guidance, and educational opportunities. Partnerships like these help us connect students with real-world inspiration, and we are incredibly grateful for their kindness and community support.

Jacqueline Johnson

Tamiyl Bradford
