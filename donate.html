Your Impact

What Your Gift Does

$25

Provides a full set of school supplies for one student — backpack, notebooks, pencils, and more.

$50

Covers nutritional support and meals for one student for an entire week.

$100

Contributes toward a tablet or device that gives a student access to technology at home.

$250

Funds individual counseling sessions for a student facing trauma or emotional challenges.

$500

Supports one full month of program delivery — academic tutoring, supplies, and wellness services — for a classroom.
